Who Are We

ELPI Coffee Co. envisions to bring local artisanal cold brew coffee to the people all over the metro and eventually in Asia. Using only the best organic beans sourced from the northern part of the country. Situated in Las Pinas, ECC has the advantage to reach out many of the business districts nearby promising you to deliver the best cold brew coffee every time.

Why Cold Brew?

  • Less Acidic

    It has approximately 67% less acidity than the hot brewed coffee. So it's a great option to those who have sensitive stomachs.

  • Tastes sweeter

    Because of the whole brewing process, it brings out a whole different flavor profile and it actually tastes sweeter because of the less acidic part.

  • Convenient

    We steep and brew our coffee for at least 18 hours and carefully extracting it via our double filtration method. Since it's in a bottle, you can enjoy our coffee anytime, anywhere, everywhere!

  • Many uses

    You can enjoy it cold, hot, with milk, frozen, flavor for your baking, see where I'm getting at? Yes, it has a LOT of use.
