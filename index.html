Who Are We

Established in 2019, ECC or ELPI Coffee Co. envisions to bring local artisanal cold brew coffee to the people all over the metro and eventually in Asia. Using only the best organic beans sourced from the northern part of the country. Situated in Las Pinas, ECC has the advantage to reach out many of the business districts nearby promising you to deliver the best cold brew coffee every time.

Why Cold Brew?

As an alternative way of brewing coffee, cold brew coffees are less acidic than its heat-brewed brother. In addition to that, coffees that are cold brewed are much more sweeter and smoother since it's not exposed to higher temperatures. Lastly, cold brews can give you a lot of flexibility on how you use or serve them. Hot or cold, or even as flavor to your baking, it's really up to you!
